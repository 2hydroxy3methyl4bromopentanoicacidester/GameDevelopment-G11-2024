SUPER SEXY MEATBALLS

Swaggy Gamer Sauce in Every Bite!

What Makes These Meatballs Swagalicious?

Step into the future of flavor with **Super Sexy Meatballs**—cyberpunk-inspired, juicy, and dripping with **Gamer Sauce**! These meatballs are packed with swagger, combining high-octane taste with a futuristic twist. Perfect for that midnight game grind or fueling your next cyber expedition.

Ingredients to Unlock Maximum Swag

  • 1 lb of cyber-charged ground beef
  • 1 lb of ultra-swaggy ground pork
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs from the Matrix
  • 1/4 cup of grated Neon Parmesan
  • 2 cloves of Hacker-grade garlic, minced
  • 1 large egg, infused with cyber-energy
  • 1 tsp salt of the cyber-seas
  • 1/2 tsp freshly cracked black pepper of the future
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning from the neon gardens
  • 1/4 cup fresh swag-parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup digital milk

How to Cook with Swag

  1. Preheat your cyber-oven to 400°F (or 200°C if you're in the cyber-EU).
  2. In a glowing neon bowl, mix all ingredients with style. Don’t overmix, or you’ll overload the swag circuits!
  3. Roll them into sleek 1.5-inch meatballs and place on a futuristic baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes until they hit peak swagger—browned and crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside.
  5. Serve with a side of pasta, dunk in **Gamer Sauce**, or eat them straight from cyberspace!
